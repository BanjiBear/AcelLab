Plan and Pricing

Startline provides multiple plans to suit you and your business’s need. All plans are charged monthly with no contracts, no setup fees, and no hidden gimmicks.


It is very important to identify what type of user you are when you create an account. Startline offers two different tracks (Innovator and Corporate) to provide different type of solutions.

Innovator:
Innovator accounts focus on showcasing company/ product information to potential clients or business partners. By utilizing the "My Innovator" section, Innovator users can fill in their company information to create Company Cards on the Search page so other users can view their company profile.

For Innovator users to have a glimps of what its like to be a corporate user, we have unlocked features of the free plan of Corporate users for Startups users to enjoy!

Corporate:
Corporate users have all features of Innovator accounts along with features to view detailed information of other companies on the search page. By utilizing this feature, Corporate users are able to look for companies in specific technology verticals that could be useful for the growth of their company.

We offer a monthly subscription service with no long-term contracts.
Users are billed monthly for the upcoming month's service. The subscription can be terminated at any time before the next billing date without any penalties or refunds.

Here's how our termination policy works:

  1. Payment Cycle: When a user signs up for our service, they are billed for the upcoming month. The subscription plan runs from the day of payment until the day before the next payment date.

  2. Refunds: Once a payment date has passed, refunds are not provided for that billing cycle. Users are responsible for the full monthly fee if they cancel after the payment date has passed.

  3. Termination: Upon cancellation, the subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle and automatic renewal will stop. Users will not be billed for the subsequent month.

The information you provide will be used to create your own company card on our platform.



CEO Contact
This section facilitates direct communication with your CEO, allowing key clients to engage with your company's leadership for valuable business opportunities.

Basic Information:
Comprising key details such as contact information, website URL, company size, founding year, industry sector, headquarters location, and descriptive tags, this section offers a comprehensive overview of your organization.

Team Information:
Enable potential customers to connect effortlessly by showcasing your dedicated team members and their roles within your company.

Product List:
Presenting detailed product information such as product photos, brochures, names, and descriptions, this section offers easy access to the array of offerings provided to your customers.

Financials: By detailing your funding stage, this section provides clients with insights into your company's stability and growth trajectory, fostering confidence in your business.
